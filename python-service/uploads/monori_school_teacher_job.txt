================================================================================

ABOUT MONORI SCHOOL

Monori School is a prestigious private college-preparatory institution serving grades 9-12, with an enrollment of 450 students. Founded in 1995, we are committed to academic excellence, character development, and preparing students for success in higher education and beyond. Our diverse student body represents 25 countries, and we maintain a student-teacher ratio of 12:1.

Our mission is to cultivate critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and lifelong learners through rigorous academics, innovative teaching, and a supportive community environment.

================================================================================

POSITION OVERVIEW

Monori School seeks an enthusiastic and dedicated High School Mathematics Teacher to join our dynamic academic team. The successful candidate will teach a range of mathematics courses from Algebra to Advanced Calculus, inspire students to develop strong mathematical reasoning skills, and contribute to our school's culture of academic excellence.

================================================================================

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Teaching & Instruction:
• Teach 4-5 mathematics courses across grades 9-12, including Algebra I/II, Geometry, Pre-Calculus, and AP Calculus
• Develop and deliver engaging, standards-aligned lesson plans that accommodate diverse learning styles
• Implement differentiated instruction strategies to meet the needs of all learners
• Integrate technology and innovative teaching methods to enhance student understanding
• Create and grade assignments, quizzes, tests, and projects that accurately assess student learning
• Maintain accurate records of student attendance, grades, and progress
• Provide timely and constructive feedback to students on their academic performance

Student Support & Development:
• Establish a positive, inclusive, and safe classroom environment conducive to learning
• Provide additional academic support through office hours and tutoring sessions
• Mentor and advise students on academic goals and college preparation
• Communicate regularly with students and families regarding academic progress and concerns
• Differentiate instruction to support students with diverse learning needs and abilities
• Encourage critical thinking, problem-solving, and mathematical reasoning skills

School Community Involvement:
• Participate in department meetings, curriculum development, and professional learning communities
• Collaborate with colleagues across disciplines on interdisciplinary projects
• Attend faculty meetings, professional development workshops, and school events
• Advise or coach at least one extracurricular activity (Math Club, Science Olympiad, etc.)
• Participate in parent-teacher conferences and school open houses
• Contribute to the school's culture of continuous improvement and innovation
• Serve on school committees as assigned (curriculum, technology, student life, etc.)

Professional Growth:
• Stay current with best practices in mathematics education and pedagogy
• Pursue ongoing professional development opportunities
• Engage in reflective practice and continuous improvement of teaching methods
• Collaborate with colleagues to share resources and instructional strategies

• Master's degree in Education, Mathematics, or related field (PREFERRED)

• Minimum 3-5 years of high school mathematics teaching experience

• Deep knowledge of mathematics content across secondary curriculum

Personal Qualities:
• Passion for teaching and inspiring young learners
• High level of professionalism and ethical conduct
• Flexibility and adaptability in a dynamic educational environment
• Strong work ethic and commitment to student success

• Cultural competency and sensitivity to diverse backgrounds
